# Register an employee for the Self-Service Portal

Learn how to onboard employees so they can sign in to the Self-Service Portal.

## Prerequisites

Make sure the Self-Service Portal is enabled in your Business Central environment. For more information, see [Set up the Self-Service Portal](self-service-setup.md).

## Register a single employee

1. Choose the ![Lightbulb that opens the Tell Me feature.](media/ui-search/search_small.png "Tell me what you want to do") icon, enter **Employees**, and then choose the related link.
1. Open the employee card for the employee you want to register.
1. In the **Private Email** field, enter the employee's private email address.

   > [!NOTE]
   > Entering the email address triggers the registration process. Within a few minutes, the employee receives an invitation email at that address.

1. Ask the employee to choose the link in the invitation email and follow the instructions to set up their account.
